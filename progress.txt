**Learnings:**
- `vp` isn't in PATH in this environment â€” use `node_modules/.bin/vp test` instead. After `bun install --cwd /workspace/repo` installs deps, the binary works.
- `vi.useFakeTimers()` + `vi.advanceTimersByTimeAsync(ms)` is the clean approach for testing timer-based races in Vitest â€” no real sleeps needed. Call `vi.useRealTimers()` in `afterEach` to avoid test pollution.
